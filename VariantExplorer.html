Ahoy there, Variant Explorers!

This website is intended to help you discover discrepancies between laboratories, and therefore maybe even empower you to resolve them. You can explore the site by clicking the blue buttons above. Each button will lead you to a different path to search. Most of the numbers that you will encounter along your pathway will provide you with the associated variants, if you click on them. Also, if you click on a variant name, you will be taken to it's ClinVar page. By the way, ClinVar provided ALL of the data used in this site. Have fun exploring, and make an impact on the world around you!
